Conversions

What is this?

This is a list of common conversions I put together.

1. Length

1 meter (m) = 3.28084 feet (ft)

1 centimeter (cm) = 0.393701 inches (in)

1 millimeter (mm) = 0.0393701 inches (in)

1 kilometer (km) = 0.621371 miles (mi)

1 inch (in) = 2.54 centimeters (cm)

1 foot (ft) = 0.3048 meters (m)

1 mile (mi) = 1.60934 kilometers (km)

2. Area

1 square meter (m²) = 10.7639 square feet (ft²)

1 square centimeter (cm²) = 0.155000 square inches (in²)

1 square kilometer (km²) = 0.386102 square miles (mi²)

1 square inch (in²) = 6.4516 square centimeters (cm²)

1 square foot (ft²) = 0.092903 square meters (m²)

1 acre = 4046.86 square meters (m²)

3. Volume

1 cubic meter (m³) = 35.3147 cubic feet (ft³)

1 liter (L) = 0.264172 gallons (US gal)

1 cubic centimeter (cm³) = 0.0610237 cubic inches (in³)

1 cubic inch (in³) = 16.3871 cubic centimeters (cm³)

1 cubic foot (ft³) = 0.0283168 cubic meters (m³)

1 gallon (US gal) = 3.78541 liters (L)

4. Mass

1 kilogram (kg) = 2.20462 pounds (lb)

1 gram (g) = 0.035274 ounces (oz)

1 metric ton (t) = 1.10231 short tons (US ton)

1 pound (lb) = 0.453592 kilograms (kg)

1 ounce (oz) = 28.3495 grams (g)

5. Force

1 newton (N) = 0.224809 pounds-force (lbf)

1 kilonewton (kN) = 224.809 pounds-force (lbf)

1 pound-force (lbf) = 4.44822 newtons (N)

6. Pressure

1 pascal (Pa) = 0.000145038 pounds per square inch (psi)

1 kilopascal (kPa) = 0.145038 pounds per square inch (psi)

1 bar = 14.5038 pounds per square inch (psi)

1 atmosphere (atm) = 101.325 kilopascals (kPa)

1 pound per square inch (psi) = 6.89476 kilopascals (kPa)

7. Energy and Work

1 joule (J) = 0.737562 foot-pounds (ft-lb)

1 kilojoule (kJ) = 0.947817 British thermal units (BTU)

1 watt-hour (Wh) = 3.6 kilojoules (kJ)

1 foot-pound (ft-lb) = 1.35582 joules (J)

1 British thermal unit (BTU) = 1.05506 kilojoules (kJ)

8. Power

1 watt (W) = 0.00134102 horsepower (hp)

1 kilowatt (kW) = 1.34102 horsepower (hp)

1 horsepower (hp) = 745.7 watts (W)

1 horsepower (hp) = 550 foot-pounds per second (ft-lb/s)

9. Temperature

Celsius to Fahrenheit: \( T_F = \frac{9}{5} T_C + 32 \)

Fahrenheit to Celsius: \( T_C = \frac{5}{9} (T_F - 32) \)

Celsius to Kelvin: \( T_K = T_C + 273.15 \)

Kelvin to Celsius: \( T_C = T_K - 273.15 \)

10. Density and Specific Weight

1 kg/m³ = 0.062428 lb/ft³

1 lb/ft³ = 16.0185 kg/m³

1 N/m³ = 0.00636588 lbf/ft³

1 lbf/ft³ = 157.087 N/m³

11. Velocity

1 meter per second (m/s) = 3.28084 feet per second (ft/s)

1 kilometer per hour (km/h) = 0.621371 miles per hour (mph)

1 foot per second (ft/s) = 0.3048 meters per second (m/s)

1 mile per hour (mph) = 1.60934 kilometers per hour (km/h)

12. Flow Rate

1 cubic meter per second (m³/s) = 35.3147 cubic feet per second (ft³/s)

1 liter per second (L/s) = 0.264172 gallons per second (gal/s)

1 cubic foot per second (ft³/s) = 0.0283168 cubic meters per second (m³/s)

1 gallon per minute (gpm) = 0.0630902 liters per second (L/s)

13. Stress and Modulus

1 pascal (Pa) = 0.000145038 pounds per square inch (psi)

1 megapascal (MPa) = 145.038 pounds per square inch (psi)

1 gigapascal (GPa) = 145038 pounds per square inch (psi)

1 psi = 6894.76 pascals (Pa)

14. Angular Measurements

1 radian (rad) = 57.2958 degrees (°)

1 degree (°) = 0.0174533 radians (rad)

1 revolution per minute (rpm) = 0.1047198 radians per second (rad/s)

15. Miscellaneous

Thermal Conductivity: 1 W/(m·K) = 0.577789 BTU/(h·ft·°F)

Heat Transfer Coefficient: 1 W/(m²·K) = 0.176110 BTU/(h·ft²·°F)

Viscosity: 1 Pa·s = 10 poise (P)

Kinematic Viscosity: 1 m²/s = 10.7639 ft²/s
